AI connection

Inside Claude.ai, "Ask AI" works automatically. Once this file is hosted elsewhere (like GitHub Pages), paste your own Anthropic API key below and it'll call Claude directly from your browser — no separate server needed.

Get a key at console.anthropic.com/settings/keys. It's stored only in this browser (never in the page's source or your repo) and is sent straight to Anthropic when you use Ask AI — nowhere else. Don't paste it on a page or device you don't trust.
